                How do I claim rewards?

                Complete entries, keep your wallet verified, and watch the reward ledger for distribution events.

                Why is my payout pending?

                Pending rewards remain queued until the next distribution cycle and final verification checks complete.

                What if my wallet is not linked?

                Use the wallet verification action in the giveaway panel so payouts can be mapped to a destination address.

                How do referrals increase my chances?

                Referral activity can add bonus entries, improve your leaderboard position, and unlock badge progress.
